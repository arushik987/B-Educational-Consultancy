Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our courses and admission process

What are the eligibility criteria for admission?

Eligibility varies by course. Generally, for undergraduate programs, you need 10+2 with minimum 50% marks. For graduate programs, a relevant bachelor's degree is required. Specific requirements are mentioned in each course description.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive placement assistance including resume building, interview preparation, and connecting students with top companies in their field of study.

Are there any scholarship opportunities?

We offer various scholarship programs based on merit, financial need, and special categories. Contact our counseling team for detailed information about available scholarships.

Can I change my course after admission?

Course changes are possible within the first semester, subject to availability of seats and meeting the eligibility criteria for the desired course.
