Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How can I book a free counseling session?

You can book a free counseling session by filling out the contact form above, calling us directly, or visiting our office. We'll schedule a convenient time for you to meet with our expert counselors.

What documents do I need for counseling?

Please bring your academic transcripts, mark sheets, identity proof, and any relevant certificates. If you have specific course or college preferences, bring that information as well.

Do you provide online counseling?

Yes, we offer online counseling sessions via video calls for students who cannot visit our office. You can request an online session when booking your appointment.

How long does the counseling process take?

The initial counseling session typically takes 1-2 hours. Follow-up sessions may be scheduled based on your needs. The entire process from initial consultation to final placement can take 2-6 months depending on the course and college.
